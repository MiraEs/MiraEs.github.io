About

It was a hot summer day - I was a high school senior taking a CS class for funsies. C# was my first. No I didn't fall in love with coding then. Years later I tinkered a bit with web development, exploring the different technologies that looked cool, vicariously living through the builders who'd come up with the next hot application or robot. It wasn't until I was accepted as an iOS developer fellow at C4Q that I seriously considered becoming a developer.

A year or so down the road now, and I get to be the builder, explorer, and constant pupil to the expanding world of technology. And maybe build robots or something.

Project - LAPZE
